Nanna's Pastry Recipe

Description

Ingredients

You will need...

  • 8oz plain flour
  • 4oz butter OR 2oz margarine and 2oz TREX (vegetable fat)
  • 1 egg
  • A little salt, to taste

Method

  1. Crack your egg into a small bowl, add a little water and beat with a fork.
  2. Mix your chosen fat with the plain flour and a little salt. Make a well in the middle add pour in your egg mixture. Mix into a ball.
  3. Once in a ball, wrap your pastry in greaseproof paper (or clingfilm!) and pop it in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  4. Now you're ready to start rolling!
