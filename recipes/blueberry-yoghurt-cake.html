Blueberry and Yoghurt Cake

Description

Taken from Donna Hay's original masterpiece, this gorgeous loaf cake has become a family favourite and a staple for gatherings and get-togethers, no matter the occassion. Made moist and light with Greek yoghurt, this easy, one-bowl recipe is perfect for bakers of all levels.

Ingredients

You will need...

  • 150g unsalted / lightly salted butter, melted
  • 220g caster sugar
  • Thick Greek yoghurt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • Lemon zest, finely grated
  • 225g self-raising flour, sifted
  • Fresh blueberries

Method

  1. Measure out and begin to melt the butter on a low-medium heat.
  2. Meanwhile, place sugar, yoghurt, eggs, zest and vanilla extract into a bowl. Add the melted butter and whisk to combine (don't overdo it!).
  3. Add the flour (using a sieve) and whisk until well-combined.
  4. Gently fold the blueberries into the mixture before transferring it into a lightly greased loaf tin lined with greaseproof paper. Smooth the surface and bake for 1 hour or until top is golden and a skewer comes out clean.
  5. Allow to cool for 10 minutes before removing the cake from the tin. Dust with icing sugar to serve.
