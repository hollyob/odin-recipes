Odin Recipes

Welcome to Odin Recipes! This is your one stop shop for all things delicious, be it sweet or savoury, breakfast, lunch or dinner.

My name is Holly, and I take recipes from my life and share them with the internet! This compilation of recipes is inspired by some of my most vivid and important memories; my butterfly cupcakes take me back to my Nanna's kitchen, while the blueberry yoghurt cake is a firm favourite at family gatherings.
